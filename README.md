# App name: CRISIS WATCH

Description: 

Our app empowers individuals to stay safe and prepared in any emergency, offering real-time alerts and checklists tailored to various crisis scenarios. If you're facing a crisis like flood, or explosive  threat, the app delivers instant access to emergency contacts, nearby bomb shelters, and guided response steps. Report activities like terrorist threat or medical emergencies with the in-app reporting feature, with AI to guide you when uncertain. Even offline, users can access checklists and emergency numbers. With preparedness plans, tutorials, and nationwide updates, this is your all-in-one safety hub.

From the unexpected to the unthinkable, Crisis Watch has your back.
